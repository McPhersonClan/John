Thomson, Mary Elizabeth

Birth Name Thomson, Mary Elizabeth
Gender female
Age at Death 80 years, 7 months, 20 days

Narrative

Mary's mother, née Poustie, died in childbirth and Mary was not happy with her father and his second wife so she ran away to her mother's family as soon as she was able, was brought up by them and apparently had little connection with the Thomsons who were one of Kyneton's earliest and best known and most active families. this information was supplied by Kath Reading (née Snowball, her granddaughter) in April 1999.
