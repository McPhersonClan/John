Birth Name McDonald, Annie

Narrative

Annie was engaged to Alec McKay for twenty years because her father was a stern, hard man who made her promise not to marry , but to look after her siblings after her mother died (in 1915). So she waited until her father died too (in 1918) before marrying in 1920 at the age of 47. This would also explain why Sybella eloped. ( information from Innes Wilson & Airlie Worrall,Dec. 2000)
