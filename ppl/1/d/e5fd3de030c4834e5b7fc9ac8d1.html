MacDonald, Christian

Birth Name MacDonald, Christian
Gender female
Age at Death 79 years, 4 months, 23 days

Narrative

Not with the family in Catlodge in 1841, but re-appears in 1861, age given as 37. With James in 1881, aged 56. Angus McPherson in " A highlander Looks Back" says " As a tiny tot i went to a Sunday school held in the homely kitchen with the blazing fire in the farmhouse of Lagg, then tenanted by a Mr James McDonald and his sister Chirsty, Mr Mcdonald instructed in both Gaelic and English, but the prayer was always said in Gaelic".
