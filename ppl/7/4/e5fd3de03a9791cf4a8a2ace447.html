McPherson, John

Birth Name McPherson, John
Gender male

Narrative

Married Janet McLean in Culreoch in 1758, but they had no children. Then had a child Hellen by Ann Davidson in 1762, but they did not marry. He married Elizabeth McPherson, the daughter of Murish in 1768 by whom he had Thomas.
