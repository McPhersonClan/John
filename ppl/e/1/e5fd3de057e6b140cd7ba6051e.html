McPherson, Donald

Birth Name McPherson, Donald
Gender male
Age at Death 59 years, 10 months, 23 days

Narrative

Donald was born in Baldow of Raits, in the parish of Alvie.
Donald & Grace were married on the same day as his brother John & his wife Elizabeth McDonald. They all emigrated to Australia on board the "Oregon", which arrrived in South Australia in November, 1851.
Donald & John operated a carrying business from Broadmeadows to the Ballarat goldfields and both appeared on the electoral roll for Broadmeadows in 1856.Donald & Grace's daughter Betsy, the only survivor of four children, was registered there in 1858, with their address given as Ruthvenfield.
Some time after that they went to New Zealand, where Donald was a punt keeper at "The Dunstan" in Otago.
They left New Zealand in 1881 to return to Kingussie where they arrived in autumn of that year. In January ,1882 Donald met with a gruesome end, being hit by the parliamentary train while returning from visiting a friend in Belleville on the railway track. He was apparently unable to hear the train because of a high wind.
