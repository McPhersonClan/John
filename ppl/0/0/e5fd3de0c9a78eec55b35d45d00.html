Rippin, Maria Collis

Birth Name Rippin, Maria Collis
Gender female
Age at Death 64 years, 1 month, 5 days

Narrative

Maria was the second child of Henry and Jane who had 9 more children, the last in 1860. She was abducted by Alfred & Betsy Rippin who were neighbours with a fondness for the child. Henry & Jane knew who had her, but did nothing about it and never heard from her again. Probably they had enough other children to worry about, but the story of the lost child persisted in the family until it surfaced by means of Ruth Moore's researches in 1988 from Charmaine Piaud of Botany whose great-grandmother Ellen was Maria's sister. Alfred & Betsy eventually took her to the Victorian goldfields where she met and married Joseph Hill. (See Ruth's book ' Mr Joseph Hill of Dunolly').
