Birth Name McPherson, John
Gender male
Age at Death 71 years, 5 months, 17 days

Narrative

Between 1865 and 1871 the McPhersons were running teams of drays between Broadmeadows and the Ballarat goldfields, also bricks from St.Arnaud.
In the electoral roll of Broadmeadows of 1856 John & Donald were both recorded as carriers, but by the time of Andrew' birth in 1860 John was recorded as 'farmer'.
