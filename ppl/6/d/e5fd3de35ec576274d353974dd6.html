Also Known As McLean, Grace
Gender female
Age at Death about 78 years, 6 months

Narrative

According to the International Genealogical Index, Grace was christened Grizel (or Girzel) on February 1, 1818 to Alexander McLean & Elspet McPherson, his wife. A girl of the same name was born to the couple & christened in Ballantian, Kingussie on July 9, 1815, so she probably died & Grace was given her name, as frequently happened, especially if she was named after her paternal grandmother as was probably the case. Elspet was born about 1793 and was married to Alexander about 1814.
Grace was noted in the Kingussie parish register on her marriage as being "in Gordonhall", indicating that her father was a tenant there.
Grace's age on the shipping records of the Oregon in 1851 was given as 31, on her daughter's birth certificate in 1858 as 41 and on her death certificate in 1896 as 75. She was not reorded in Kingussie & Insch in the 1891 census as her daughter Betsy was and the only Grace McPherson of suitable age, or anything approaching it, in Scotland was recorded in the Gorbals in Glasgow, so perhaps she was visiting there, or even in England, the records of which I have not consulted. The age of that Grace was 70, so if this was indeed Donald's widow, there is yet another discrepancy.
