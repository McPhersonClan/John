John McPherson Family & others Tree
In an effort to see the late Ida Mary Stuart Dowty nee (McPherson) 1936-2002 hard work endure and updated the concept to publish the Tree on the internet came about and so we have these web pages.

Hope you enjoy these pages and your assistance would be appreciated to help update and progress the information within this Family Tree. Mostly the tree has sat dormant for almost twenty years so there are many new additions that need catching up with and It is desired that relatives can identify the gaps and respond with their family details.

The information can be about a huge number of events including simple notes and or pictures although as a guide the basic things are:-

Full Birth Name & Sex __________________ Name called and or Nick Name
Birth Date and Place ___________________ Marriage date and Place
Marriage/partner full name (maiden) _______ Death Date and place
Relationships ie Father, Mother, Daughter, Son, Step Child and Sister.

To protect people’s privacy the dates and notes of living people are not published on the web.

Details can be sent to
Keith McPherson - email - keith4mc-cma@yahoo.com.au
and please put “Family Tree” in the subject line to sort out rubbish emails.

At this stage the publishing has been an individual effort should anyone have an interest in helping with the family tree I am open for collaboration. This could be with any aspect in the upkeep to the family tree, please make contact if you are willing to help out.

It’s hopeful that you appreciate the publishing of the family tree in this basic format. The loose plan at this stage is to update the tree at its current sight when significant additions are made so please check-in again sometime. Optimistically these pages will remain at this site for a very long time.
